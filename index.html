Scoring Method
Score = weighted average of all logged lift scores. Each lift is benchmarked against strength standards relative to bodyweight. A 130kg bench + 150kg squat at ~80kg bodyweight places solidly in Platinum territory. Elite lifters (2× BW squat, 1.5× BW bench) reach Diamond and Elite.
